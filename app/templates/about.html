About Us
Advance scouting can be a challenge for college baseball coaches. Statman Sprays makes it easier.

Using publically available play-by-play data and a specifically tailored algorithm, Statman Sprays offers free, accurate spray charts for any NCAA baseball player dating back to 2018.

Use Statman Sprays to understand your opponents and efficiently position your defenders.
How does it work?
Statman Sprays pulls rosters and play-by-play data for any team in the country straight from the NCAA stats repository. That data is cleaned and parsed by our proprietary algorithm before being made available to Statman Sprays users.

Statman Sprays was developed by a former D3 student manager and current professional software developer who has spent years refining this process to bring you the most accurate and complete data possible.
