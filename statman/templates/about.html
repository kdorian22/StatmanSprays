About Us
Advance scouting can be a challenge for college baseball coaches. Statman Sprays makes it easier.

Using publically available play-by-play data and a specifically tailored algorithm, Statman Sprays offers free, accurate spray charts for any NCAA baseball player.

Use Statman Sprays to understand your opponents and efficiently position your defenders.
How does it work?
Statman Sprays pulls rosters and play-by-play data for any team in the country straight from the NCAA stats website. Those data are cleaned and parsed by our proprietary algorithm before being made available to Statman Sprays users.

Statman Sprays was developed by a former D3 student manager and current professional software developer who has spent years refining this process to bring you the most accurate and complete data possible.

You have total control:
  • Search for any NCAA team using the search bar in the top right of the SPRAYS tab.
  • To keep from filling our database with unncessary information, we allow our users to pull only the rosters and games they need.
  • The table at the top shows which information has already be pulled from the NCAA website. Hover over the ↺ icons to see when it was last updated.
  • Click on a ↺ icon to pull that data into our database. This only needs to be done once.
  • If someone else has already loaded the data you are looking for, click on a player's name to see his spray chart.
  • Use the headers on the top table to navigate between years.
  • Take a look at the Update DB tab for an overview of what has already been pulled into our database.
