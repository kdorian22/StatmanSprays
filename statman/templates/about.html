About Us
Statman Sprays makes college baseball scouting easier.

Using publically available play-by-play data and a specifically tailored algorithm, Statman Sprays offers free, accurate spray charts for any NCAA baseball player.

Use Statman Sprays to understand your opponents and efficiently position your defenders.
How does it work?
Statman Sprays pulls rosters, play-by-play data, and offensive statistics for every team in the country straight from the NCAA stats website. Those data are cleaned and parsed by our proprietary algorithm before being made available to Statman Sprays users.

Statman Sprays was developed by a former D3 student manager and current professional software developer who has spent years refining this process to bring you the most accurate and complete data possible.

Not only is our database pre-filled with every available game since the start of the 2018 season, but we also pull in new data nightly on a 24-hour delay during the season so you are always working with the most up to date information.
